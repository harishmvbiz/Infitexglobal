Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your personal information — written in plain language.

Last updated: 2026. This is a general template provided for your new business and should be reviewed by a qualified adviser before you rely on it. Replace bracketed placeholders with your real details.

INFITEX GLOBAL ADVISORY PRIVATE LIMITED ("INFITEX", "we", "us" or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle personal information in line with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). Where we operate in India, we also act consistently with applicable local data-protection requirements.

1. What we collect

We only collect personal information we reasonably need to provide our services, including:

  • Identity and contact details (name, email, phone)
  • Information you give us through forms, email, WhatsApp or calls
  • Business information relevant to an engagement
  • Basic technical data when you visit our website (for example, pages viewed), where applicable

2. How we collect it

We collect information directly from you — when you submit our contact form, email us, message us on WhatsApp, or speak with us. We do not buy personal information from third parties.

3. Why we use it

We use personal information to respond to your enquiry, provide and manage our services, meet our legal and regulatory obligations, and improve how we work. We will not use your information for unrelated purposes without your consent.

4. Marketing & your consent

We will only send you marketing communications if you have consented, and every message will include a simple way to unsubscribe, consistent with the Spam Act 2003 (Cth). You can withdraw consent at any time by contacting us.

5. Disclosure

We may share information with trusted service providers who help us operate (for example, email, form-handling and hosting providers), and with licensed professionals (such as tax agents or auditors) where an engagement requires it. We require these parties to protect your information. We do not sell your personal information.

6. Overseas disclosure (APP 8)

Because we operate across Australia, India and the UAE and use reputable online tools, some information may be stored or processed overseas (for example, on cloud services). We take reasonable steps to ensure any overseas recipient handles your information consistently with the APPs.

7. Storage & security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, loss and unauthorised access, including secure (HTTPS) connections and access controls. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to reduce risk.

8. Data breaches

If a data breach occurs that is likely to result in serious harm, we will respond in line with the Notifiable Data Breaches scheme, including notifying affected individuals and the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) where required.

9. Access & correction

You may request access to the personal information we hold about you, and ask us to correct it if it is inaccurate. Contact us at info@infitexglobal.com and we will respond within a reasonable time.

10. Complaints

If you believe we have mishandled your information, please contact us first at info@infitexglobal.com so we can investigate. If you are not satisfied with our response, you may contact the OAIC at oaic.gov.au.

11. Cookies & analytics

Our website may use minimal cookies or privacy-respecting analytics to understand general usage. Where required, we will seek your consent. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version will always be available on this page with its "last updated" date.
